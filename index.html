Meet the Owner

Rich Schafenacker

BSc. MBA · Founder & Head Fitter

Rich founded Cycle-Logic in 1988 and has spent over 40 years living and breathing competitive cycling. As a former national level road and track racer once a member of the celebrated Proctor Team — he went on to sponsor a squad that podiumed at the World Championships, Commonwealth Games, and PanAm Games.

A self described diehard racer, Rich has also competed in multiple IronMan events worldwide and represented Canada at the 2017 Masters Track World Championships. His depth of experience from the saddle, the workshop, and the coaching box — is what makes every bike fit and every recommendation at Cycle-Logic genuinely different.
